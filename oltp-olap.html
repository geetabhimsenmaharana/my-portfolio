OLTP vs OLAP, Normalization & SCD

Core Data Engineering Concepts

1. OLTP vs OLAP

OLTP — Running the Business

OLTP systems handle day-to-day transactions like bookings, updates, and payments.

orders (OLTP table)

order_id | customer_id | service | status

101 | 1 | Termite | Completed

Purpose: Store and update live business activity

OLAP — Understanding the Business

OLAP systems are used for analytics and reporting.

  • Revenue by city
  • Monthly service trends
  • Technician performance

Purpose: Analyze business performance

OLTP captures data → OLAP analyzes data

2. Normalization vs Denormalization

Normalization (OLTP)

Data is split to avoid duplication and improve integrity.
